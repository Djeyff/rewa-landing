Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Retena ("we", "our", "us") provides team communication intelligence services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you connect your organization to Retena, we collect your organization name, administrator contact details, and the identifiers necessary to link your messaging platform account to our service.

Communication Data: We capture messages and voice notes from the group conversations you have explicitly authorized. Voice recordings are transcribed in real-time and the original audio is immediately discarded after processing — we never store raw audio files.

Transcription & Message History: The text content of transcribed voice notes and captured messages is stored in your organization's dedicated workspace to enable search and recall features.

Usage Data: We collect metadata such as message counts, transcription volumes, and feature usage to manage service limits and billing.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide searchable organizational memory and AI-powered recall
  • To transcribe voice notes and make them text-searchable
  • To generate daily summaries and team activity insights
  • To enforce usage limits based on your subscription tier
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To send service-related notifications and alerts

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • AI Processing: Voice notes are sent to our speech-to-text infrastructure for transcription. Audio is processed transiently and not used for model training.
  • AI Search & Summaries: Message content is sent to our AI providers for query answering and summarization. This data is processed in real-time and is not used to train external models.
  • Payment Processing: Billing information is handled by our payment processor. We never store credit card numbers.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our rights, provided we notify you where legally permissible.

4. Data Retention

  • Audio files: Deleted immediately after transcription. Never stored.
  • Messages & transcriptions: Retained for the period specified in your subscription (6 months for Starter, 12 months for Pro, unlimited for Business). You may request deletion at any time.
  • Account data: Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account cancellation request.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (TLS 1.3), encrypted data at rest, access controls, and regular security reviews. Your team's communication data is isolated in a dedicated workspace and not accessible to other organizations.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold for your organization
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your workspace and all associated data
  • Export: Export your transcription history (Pro and Business tiers)
  • Portability: Your data belongs to you — not us

7. Consent & Team Members

By adding Retena to a group, the account administrator represents that all group members have been informed that their messages and voice notes will be captured and analyzed. We recommend informing your team before activating Retena in any group.

8. Children's Privacy

Retena is a B2B service intended for business use only. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18 years of age.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us or email privacy@retena.app.
