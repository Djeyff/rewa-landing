FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How does Retena connect to my WhatsApp groups?+
Add Retena's WhatsApp number to any existing group as a participant. Retena instantly starts capturing and transcribing all voice notes. No apps, no config. Setup is under 2 minutes.
Does my team need to install anything?+
No. Your team keeps using WhatsApp exactly as they always have. Retena is invisible — it just listens in the background. Only the manager/admin needs to access the Retena dashboard.
What happens to the audio files?+
Audio is processed in real-time and immediately deleted. We never store voice recordings — only the text transcription, encrypted at rest. Never used to train AI models.
What languages does Retena support?+
99 languages with automatic detection. Each voice note is detected and transcribed in its own language. Mixed-language teams work perfectly — Spanish, French, Portuguese, Arabic, English, German, and 93 more.
Is Retena legal to use?+
Yes. Retena appears as a visible participant in the group — your team can see it's there. We recommend informing your team. We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and local data protection laws.
Can I try Retena before paying?+
Yes. Every plan includes a 7-day free trial with no credit card. Early access members lock their rate forever.
Why per team, not per seat?+
Because your whole team benefits — not just the person paying. A team of 15 shouldn't pay 15 × $19/seat. Retena scales with the number of groups you manage, not headcount.
What's the difference between Retena and VozClara?+
VozClara is personal: you transcribe YOUR OWN voice notes. Retena is for teams: it captures EVERYONE's voice notes in WhatsApp groups. Different tools, different use cases — many users have both.
